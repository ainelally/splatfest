ABOUT ME

Hi I'm IV! I'm 22 and I have been playing Splatoon ever since 2015, and I am still playing the series to this day. Ever since I picked up the Wii U game back then I fell in love with the setting, the characters, the lore, the music, the gameplay and pretty much everything about this franchise. I made this website because I want to share information about the game and because I have a passion for the series. For example as you can see on the navigation bar: information about the abilities you can get on your gear and which gear is more likely to give certain abilities you want and a resource page which has link on where I get Splatoon information from!

Currently in Splatoon 3 I am level 27 and my catalog level is 66. I am a B- rank in Anarchy Battles. I primarily play Turf War and Salmon Run especially but on the odd occasion I play in Anarchy (but only on Open and on anything but Clam Blitz). I typically wear the Berry BlobMob shirt, Squid Hairclip and Slamgerine Slip-Ons. In both Splatoon 1 and 2 I LOVED using the NZAP and Aerospray family but now in Splatoon 3 I have been trying out new weapon classes. I like to use the Splatlings now and the Dualies. My favourite in-game brand is Emberz and Toni Kensa.
